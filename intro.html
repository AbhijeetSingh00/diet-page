Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines eggs, cheese, and your choice of fillings.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread evenly.
  4. When nearly set, add fillings on one half.
  5. Fold the omelette and serve immediately.
